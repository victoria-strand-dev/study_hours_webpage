What is StudyHours?

As a student I often struggle to delegate my time. There's several different courses at the same time, that don't necessarily have that much in common, and there's a lot of assigments. As most people I want to do other things as well. The more things there are to do, the harder it is to keep track of everything.

If you want good grades, or at least pass, you need to put in the work - and have a plan.

I don't know how many times I've tried to make good plans that actually work and are realistic, but I know it's way too many. Using more time on planning than actually studying is a problem I think many other students, and people in general, also struggle with.

So, how do you make a study plan that actually works?

After all my efforts, I believe I have found a way. For me personally, getting straight A's is not the goal. I don't want to always be on the verge of failing either. Therefore I started to calculate how much time I needed to do to get a spesific grade in each spesific course - and which grades I could expect based on the time I used on each course. In practice, it's not that simple since we all learn different topics in different speeds, but it's a good indicator.
